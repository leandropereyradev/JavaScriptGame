Wonder Ghost

In this adventure, our Wonder Ghost must free his bat friends, captured in experimental cages. Let's help our Ghosthero to face mummies and Rockstars zombies, created in the laboratory of the most feared villain of all times:

Dr. Julius Motauski

We accompany our hero through the cemetery to free his bat friends trapped in cages. Our Wonder Ghost throws energy SpiritBombs to defend itself.!

At the end of this dark journey, we will help Wonder Ghost to unleash the most epic battle ever told against Dr. Julius Motauski. Thousands of bats trapped for future experiments will have to be released. Join and we will win!
